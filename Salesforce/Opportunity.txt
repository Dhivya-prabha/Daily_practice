1. Login to https://login.salesforce.com/?locale=in
2. Click on menu button from the right corner
3. Click view All and click Sales from App Launcher
4. Click on the arrow mark of Opportunity and click New Opportunity
5. Type Opportunity Name in New Opportunity window
6. Select close date as tomorrow
7. Select stage as Value Proposition
8. Click Save button
9. Click on Details and verify the Opportunity Name and Stage
